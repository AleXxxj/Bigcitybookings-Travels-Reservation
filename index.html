Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a flight? ▼
Simply fill out the booking form above or send us a message on WhatsApp. We'll confirm availability and send you payment details. Once confirmed, we email your e-ticket immediately.
Do you handle last-minute travel? ▼
Yes! Emergency travel is one of our specialties. Contact us anytime, and we'll work fast to get you booked even on short notice.
What airlines do you work with? ▼
We work with all major airlines including Air Peace, Dana Air, Arik Air, Emirates, Qatar Airways, British Airways, Ethiopian Airlines, and many more.
Do you offer hotel reservations too? ▼
Absolutely! We book hotels worldwide. Just let us know your destination and preferences.
How do I pay? ▼
We accept bank transfers and cash deposits. All payment details are shared after booking confirmation.
